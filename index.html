ABOUT US

TABIBU is open to all patients every day and provides fundamental medical care and cutting-edge medicine in a central location in Nairobi. We use our superior academic knowledge to treat a wide range of health issues, taking a personal touch and utilizing highly specialized and up-to-date research.

DID YOU KNOW

They are seriously understaffed. Your nurse probably isn’t taking her sweet time bring your ice water, she’s likely tending to an emergency. Things like food and beverages are a relatively low priority. If it’s something that a nurse’s assistant can easily bring, please considering asking them before you ask your nurse.

Nurses can’t administer medications without a physician’s order. If you ask for a cough drop, expect that it’s going to take at least an hour. The physician will have to be paged. Pharmacy will have to review the order. All of that has to happen before the medication can be administered to you.
